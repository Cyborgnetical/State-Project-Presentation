History

Beginning

Mississippi Territory

Alabama entered the USA as the Mississippi territory in 1798.

The territory was expanded in 1804 and 1812 as the USA Annexed more land

Finally the Mississippi Territory split into the state of Mississippi and the Alabama Territory

State Hood

December 14, 1819

Alabama came out of the Mississippi territory because white southerners wanted to see 2 slave states emerge

Under President James Monroe Alabama became a state with William Wyatt Bibb as its first governor
